Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 18, 2024

At Alibi ("we," "our," or "us"), located at joinalibi.com, we understand the deeply personal nature of your thoughts, notes, and dictations. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting the sensitive data and creative content you entrust to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information.

1. Information We Collect

We only collect the information necessary to provide our service:

  • Account Data: Information you provide when creating an account, such as your email address and basic profile information.
  • Your Content (The Sensitive Stuff): The text notes, audio dictations (voice), project data, ideas, and drafts you create, submit, or store within Alibi. This is your intellectual property.
  • Usage Data: Basic analytics on how you interact with the app to help us identify bugs and improve performance.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information exclusively to provide, maintain, and improve the Alibi service. Specifically, we process your audio and text entries via secure AI systems to extract insights, format your ideas, and generate drafts strictly on your behalf.

3. Zero Data Selling Policy

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data, audio recordings, or transcripts to any third parties under any circumstances.

Your data is strictly treated as yours. We share data only with essential third-party service providers (like secure cloud hosting and AI language models) strictly for the operational purpose of running Alibi. These providers act purely as data processors—they are legally bound to not use your data for their own independent purposes, nor are they permitted to use your sensitive data to train their public foundation models.

4. Data Security

We implement robust industry-standard security measures to protect your sensitive recordings, notes, and drafts against unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. Access to your data is secured through strict authentication protocols.

5. Your Rights and Ownership

You retain 100% full ownership over all the content you create, dictate, or generate on Alibi. You have the right to:

  • Access and review all your personal data and content.
  • Edit or update your information.
  • Export your data at any time.
  • Permanently delete your account and all associated data, files, and audio recordings from our servers.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or how your sensitive data is handled, please contact us at privacy@joinalibi.com.
